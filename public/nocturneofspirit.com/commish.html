I am currently accepting commissions.

As a freelance artist, I do work for hire. I specialize in computer-colored, pencil, and acrylic works. If you want an illustration done for a book, game, website, or whathaveyou, I'm the guy to talk to.

I determine price on a case-by-case basis, based on complexity of the work and expense required for the acquisition of materials. Naturally, then, paintings will be more expensive than computer-colored works.

Bulk orders (i.e., more than 3 images of the same type) will usually be cheaper per item than a single image.
