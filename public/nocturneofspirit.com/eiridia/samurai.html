Samurai

One of the most important elements in holding certain kingdoms together following the most recent Apocalypse was the Samurai, the honor-bound and virtuous warrior. They are masters of melee combat, and the most stable element in many societies.

The first love of a Samurai is always honor. If honor demands she kill a family member or hated enemy, she will do so. There are no exceptions (well, not officially anyway; those that give up honor become outcasts, Ronin). Samurai live by bushido - the Way of the Warrior. Certain principles must be upheld:

1) Never harm an innocent
2) Always obey the King and your superiors
3) Lie only to the enemy
4) Strive to be pure of mind, body, and soul
5) Always accept a duel challenge
6) Never attack from behind
7) Never use ranged weapons, ESPECIALLY not guns

If even one of these tenets is violated, the offender must satisfy the requirements of honor - usually ritual suicide.

While Samurai may seem overzealous at times, they are generally highly effective and well-regarded by the populace. Most Samurai belong to a Noble House; those few who do not are either specially appointed by the King/Emperor/whatever, Ronin, or worst of all, pretenders. Both Ronin and pretenders are slain on sight as the worst sort of criminal.

Game Stats

 Initial Equipment
1 Light Weapon of choice
1 Light Armor of choice
2 sets of Noble Clothing
1d10x5 gold coins

Special Abilities
Get one of the following as a special Class Ability:

Reflexes - Add +2 to Attack Dice to reflect highly-trained combat ability.

Ronin - May ignore honor and bushido, but is hunted by other Samurai. +10 Creation Points.

Ryusendo - The Way of the Dragon Blade, this is a special school of training for Samurai. Those who graduate are especially ferocious fighters, gaining +100% to all damage done by them.

Hitokagedo - The Way of the Shadow, another Samurai dojo. Graduates are known for their deadly silence. Can ignore the "never attack from behind" honor rule, and can make a special Stealth check (+50% to an Athletics check) to avoid being seen/heard. This Stealth check can be done to "disappear" as well.

Honoukendo - The Way of the Flaming Sword, source of the zealous "Bearers of the Flame." These Samurai take honor to a religious level, and seek out those who don't follow Bushido - even if they aren't Samurai - and force them to follow. Due to their dedication, graduates of this school have +25% to Intimidation checks and +2 to Willpower (in addition to the normal Samurai bonus, may not exceed 10).


Other Notes
All Samurai get a +1 bonus to Willpower.
Each Special Ability may be increased by an amount specified by the GM with Experience.

Character Point Cost: 15
