Vulture

Especially after the Third Apocalypse, raiders and bandits have become commonplace. One particular breed of these rogues, the "Vultures," are more plentiful than others. They are scavengers, scouring the countryside and collecting artifacts of before the Apocalypse (be they forgotten or in the hands of others). Most often they sell these rare items, acquiring sometimes huge sums of money or an equivalent in exchange.

The Vulture does not frequently run in a pack; he prefers to go it solo, since partners can't be trusted. He's out to protect the interests of #1 - himself. He could care less about what the artifacts are; he just wants the cash.

Vultures are common everywhere. They run the gamut from well-armed, well-armored vulture bands to solo treasure hunters.

Game Stats

 Initial Equipment
1 Light Weapon of choice
1 Light Armor of choice
1 set of travelling clothes

Special Abilities
Get one of the following as a special Class Ability:

Appraisal - allows the character a bonus of +50% to any History checks made to determine the value of an item. Example: History score of 4, Appraisal check of 6 (50% of History = 2, 4+2=6). Round up.

Ratrace - the character is unusually fast and dextrous. Add +1 to Dexterity, and +1 to Dodge skill. May not exceed 10 maximum. This is in addition to the normal vulture Dexterity bonus.

Knifemaster - vultures are known for their skill in knife-fighting. This ability adds +2 to the character's Melee skill when using knives.

Cunning - sometimes a vulture just KNOWS the way out of a situation. He adds +50% to his Intuition skill checks when under pressure.


Other Notes
All vultures gain a +1 bonus to Dexterity.
Each Special Ability may be increased by an amount specified by the GM with Experience.

Character Point Cost: 10
