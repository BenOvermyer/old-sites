Zhai'helleva, traveler, and welcome to my profile page. It is here that I will provide you with a small amount of information relating to myself; nothing TOO frightening, I assure you.

I am, and always have been, an artist. It took me 15 years, however, to realize that. Once I did, everything fell into place and my life's been mostly smooth sailing since then. Going to a decent art school in New Zealand right now...called Otago Polytechnic School of Art, and for a polytech, it's surprisingly good. Unfortunately, it uses the New Zealand/British system of grading, which absolutely sucks a$$.

Once I get my BFA from here, I'm going to either enter the computer game industry or the movie industry, perhaps in New Zealand.

Coming soon...my resume!
