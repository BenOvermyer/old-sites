Songwind



Songwind's works





Clan Status: scout/mage

Mage Status: Journeyman

Bondbird: Talahn, a great horned owl

Description: She has long, flowing blonde hair, often worn in elaborate braids. Wears elaborate, yet practical clothing, often in shades of green. Loves wearing elaborate jewelry.

Demeanor: Songwind is a night-owl with a good sense of humor. However, when needed, she is very serious, and will not joke at all. When anyone is in a bad mood, she will make an attempt to cheer them up, and is usually successful. She is always grouchy in the morning, but becomes more lighthearted as the day progresses.

Abilities: Empathy, and a very strong mage gift. She is also very good with dagger and bow.

Hobbies: Songwind loves making music, and often plays for others. She also enjoys making jewelry out of beads in her spare time.

Ekele Location: High in the branches of a huge tree in the northernmost part of the Vale.
