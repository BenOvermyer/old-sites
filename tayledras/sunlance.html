Mage Status: Adept

Clan Status:Scout/Mage

Description: A young mage of about 18-20 years of age. He stands to be about 6' 3" tall. He has long, silver hair that goes down to be just above waist length, and he has silver eye's, matching the hair. Although he is a mage, he is rather muscular from working as a scout for a very long time. He wears the scouts clothing of close fitted tunic and trousers.

Demeanor: He is a very kind and gentle person, but he can be rough and evil if he needs to.

Abilities: Adept mage skills, as well as a little knowledge with the bow & arrow.

Hobbies: He likes to carve wooden images when he has free time.

Ekele Location: He lives right outside the Veil, pretty close to where his scouting position is.
